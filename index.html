Fireborne is a world-building God-Game for PC, targeted for release in 2015.

You are the God of Fire and Creation, and only you can bring light back to your home- world of Omen. Push back the darkness, step by step. Salvage what life remains, and restore it to the land. Expand your reach, shape the earth to your will, and find the surviving Yin, for they are your most important asset in The War.

Rebuild their world.

Find them relics and blades of power.

Teach them magic and the Ancestor Tongue, that they might wake their brethren from the dead to lend what aid they can.

Because the Greed is a stranger to Fold, and this land belongs to you.
